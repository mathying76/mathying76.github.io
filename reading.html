Multi-task learning reading list


Time: Thursday 11:30 am-12:30 pm; Avenue: Room 805, Dept. of Computer Science, University College London (unless informed specially).

Thursday 16/11: Structured learning

From 11:00am--12:30pm, Yiming will talk about PAC-Bayesian bounds for structured learning:

  • P. L. Bartlett, M. Collins, D. McAllester, and B. Taskar, Large margin methods for structured classification: Exponential gradient algorithms and PAC-Bayesian generalization bounds. (mainly section 5)

  • J. Langford and J. Shawe-Taylor, Pac-Bayes and margins, NIPS, 2002.

  • D. McAllester, Simplified PAC-Bayesian margin bounds, COLT, 2003.

Thursday 14/9: Statistical learning theory

From 11:30am--12:30pm, Yiming will talk about the Rademacher averages and Dudley's integrals used in generalization error analysis.

Friday 16/6: Multi-task feature learning

From 11:00am--12:30pm, Massi will present their recent results on multi-task feature learning.
